NEBC Teens

About NEBC Teens

Who We Are, What We Stand For, and Why We Exist

Our Mission

Raising a generation of spiritually strong, confident, purpose-driven teenagers who passionately follow Jesus and positively impact their world.

Our Vision

To build teens who love God, love people, and shape the future with excellence through authentic faith, meaningful relationships, and purposeful living.

Our Core Values

Faith in God

Building our lives on the foundation of God's Word and growing in relationship with Jesus.

Love & Community

Creating a welcoming space where everyone belongs and experiences authentic relationships.

Purpose & Identity

Helping teens discover their God-given purpose and walk confidently in their identity in Christ.

Excellence

Doing everything with excellence as an act of worship and testimony to God's goodness.

Service & Leadership

Equipping teens to serve others and lead with humility, integrity, and compassion.

Integrity

Living with honesty and consistency in both public and private aspects of life.

Spiritual Growth

Pursuing continuous growth in faith, character, and relationship with God.

What Makes Us Different

Strong Spiritual Grounding

We don't just entertain teens—we disciple them. Our teachings are biblically grounded and practically applied to help teens navigate real-life challenges with faith and wisdom.

Creative Teen Activities

From game nights to creative arts, we design engaging activities that help teens connect, have fun, and build meaningful relationships in a safe environment.

Real-Life Mentorship

Our leaders aren't just event organizers—they're mentors who invest in teens' lives, offering guidance, support, and a listening ear through the ups and downs of teenage years.

Safe Space for Questions

We welcome tough questions and honest doubts. Our environment encourages spiritual exploration and growth without judgment or pressure.

Talent Development

We help teens discover and develop their God-given talents through worship teams, media ministry, creative arts, and leadership opportunities.

Community Impact

We believe faith should be lived out. Our teens participate in local outreach and service projects, making a tangible difference in our community.

Our Journey

2015
NEBC Teens Ministry Launched

Started with just 12 teens meeting in a small room, focused on creating authentic community.

2017
First Summer Retreat

Took 35 teens to our first overnight retreat, creating lasting memories and spiritual breakthroughs.

2019
Teen Worship Team Formed

Launched our teen-led worship team, empowering young musicians and vocalists.

2021
Outreach Program Started

Began regular community service projects, teaching teens to live out their faith through action.

2023
Record Attendance

Celebrated serving over 100 teens weekly with multiple small groups and ministry teams.
